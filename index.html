About Me

I'm a young developer and designer diving deep into the world of low-level systems and game development. My curiosity drives me to understand how things work at their core, which is why I'm currently learning Assembly and exploring hardware-level programming.

For me, technology is about empowerment and accessibility. I'm a strong advocate for open-source software because I believe in the power of community-driven development. When I'm not coding, you'll find me designing game mechanics or exploring new ways to create engaging user experiences.

In everything I create, I prioritize user privacy and ethical design. I believe technology should serve people, not exploit them. My goal is to build tools and experiences that respect users while pushing technical boundaries.
